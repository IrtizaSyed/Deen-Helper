JDC Welfare Organization is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving without any discrimination of caste, creed, and color. We believe that everyone deserves equality, food, shelter, access to education, & healthcare facilities and it is our goal to work towards creating a world where equality triumphs.

About Deen Helpers (NGO)

Charity Efforts Are For Happiness

Since our inception in 2009, from a small camp that provided rescue and disaster management services. Expanded its branches and reach out to an effective infrastructure that deals with social services, and welfare nationwide. To manifest this into reality we have been working tirelessly, currently, we have 89 successful projects facilitating people in sectors including health, poverty alleviation, disaster management, food, shelter, education & social causes. Our team of dedicated professionals and volunteers are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of those we serve.
